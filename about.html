How The Club Started

Our Tech Club started with a simple idea. A small group of students shared the same curiosity about technology and how digital tools shape the world. These students wanted a place where they could learn new skills together, explore modern technology, and build projects that solve real problems.

With the support of teachers and mentors, the club slowly grew into a vibrant community of learners. Students from different classes and academic backgrounds joined with the same goal of learning, building, and sharing knowledge. Today the club stands as a space where students explore creativity through technology while helping one another grow.

Disciplines Students Explore

The Tech Club introduces students to different areas in the world of technology. Each discipline gives members the opportunity to learn practical skills and discover possible future careers.


They include:

  1. Web Development
  2. Programming and Software Development
  3. Robotics and Embedded Systems
  4. Cyber Security Awareness
  5. Artificial Intelligence Basics
  6. Digital Design and User Interface Design

Team Collaboration

Working as a team is an important part of the club experience. Members often form small groups to build projects together. During these sessions students learn how to share ideas, plan tasks, and support each other while building solutions.

Through collaboration students also develop communication skills, leadership confidence, and the ability to work in diverse teams. These skills are very valuable in the modern technology industry.
